EcoYouth Nonprofit – Organization Overview

Demo document for GrantWeave onboarding (501(c)(3) / Pitch Deck)

Mission

To empower underrepresented youth through climate education and sustainable community projects in urban areas.

Programs

• Youth Climate Leaders Fellowship – training and mentorship for 14–22 year olds.
• Urban Garden Initiative – school and community gardens with curriculum tie-ins.
• Community Resilience Workshops – local preparedness and sustainability skills.
